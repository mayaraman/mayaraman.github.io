ABOUT THE CHEF
Priya Raghuvir was born and raised in Chennai, India, growing up around all of the rich Indian culture. She learned the beginnings of classic Indian dishes from her childhood in Chennai. After marriage, she immigrated to the United States with her husband. She gave birth to two daughters two years apart. She picked up on and had tried much Indian food during her childhood, but did not prepare it herself. Upon beginning to cook in the United States, she quickly adapted and even now throws in her own twists and turns to the food.
