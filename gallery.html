Milk Halwa

A rich Indian sweet made for festive occasions

Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

2 cups full fat milk (1/2 litre)

1/3 cup sugar

1 tbsp sooji

1 tbsp curd

3 tbsp ghee

1/8 tsp cardamom powder

Instructions

1. Boil milk & reduce until it becomes almost 1/3rd.

2. Add sugar, mix well then add sooji.

3. Cook until it starts to thicken. At this stage add curd.

4. Milk will start to split, keep cooking in low flame.Keep stirring to avoid burning at the bottom. It will start to thicken and stick.

5. Start adding ghee & stir continuously

6. When it sticks add ghee in intervals and keep cooking.

7. Sprinkle cardamom powder, mix well, and switch off flame.

Methi Corn Masala

A light, creamy gravy that serves as accompaniment for rice, naan, or bread

Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

3/4 cup chopped fenugreek

1 1/2 cups boiled sweet corn kernels

3/4 cup thinly sliced onions

1 cup milk

2 tbsp chopped cashewnuts

1/2 tsp finely chopped garlic

1/2 cup fresh yoghurt

2 tsp bengal gram flour

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

2 tbsp oil

1/2 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp green chilli paste

salt to taste

Instructions

1. Combine the onions, milk, cashews and garlic in a deep kadhai, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 4 to 5 minutes or till the onions turn soft.

2. Cool and blend to a smooth paste.

3. Combine yoghurt, bengal gram flour, turmeric powder and 3 tbsp of water in a bowl and whisk well. Keep aside.

4. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan, add the cumin seeds.

5. When the seeds crackle, add the fenugreek leaves, coriander powder and 2 tbsp of water, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute, while stirring continuously.

7. Serve immediately!

Apple Thokku (Pickle)

A spicy, tangy pickle that serves as an accompaniment for yogurt rice, roti, or bread

Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

2 green apples

1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

1/2 teaspoon red chili powder

A pinch of asafoetida, garlic powder, or onion powder

Salt, to taste

1/2 teaspoon cumin powder

1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds

1 teaspoon mustard seeds

1/4 cup, plus 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions

1. Chop apples & grate them into medium texture

2. Heat oil in a pan or kadhai. Temper mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and asafoetida. Add grated apple and stir well.

3. Now, add turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt to taste. Mix well everything and cook for 15-18 minutes till all the moisture is absorbed from apple and you get a thick mixture. Store in an airtight container.
